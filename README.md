# Generational Health Drift Systematic Review
This repository contains supplementary material for a systematic review of health and morbidity comparisons across the British birth cohort studies at similar ages. 

## Working paper 
Gimeno L, Moreno-Agostino D, Danka M, Guo Y, Goisis A, Dowd JB and Ploubidis GB (2025). The Generational Health Drift: A Systematic Review of Evidence from the British Birth Cohort Studies. *MedRxiv*. doi: [10.1101/2025.06.12.25329414](https://doi.org/10.1101/2025.06.12.25329414)

## Files deposited here: 

- PRISMA Checklist
- List of papers excluded from systematic review at the full-paper screening stage
- Data extraction from included studies
- Quality assessment from included studies
- Summary of evidence from published papers on trends in age-adjusted, age-specific or age-standardised incidence
